Ceres, which orbits the Sun in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, is a unique body in the Solar System, bearing many similarities to Jupiter's moon Europa and Saturn's moon Enceladus, both considered to be potential sources for harboring life. This March, 2015, the Dawn mission will arrive at Ceres, the first of the smaller class of planets to be discovered and the closest to Earth.
