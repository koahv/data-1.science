>(ESA/Hubble Information Centre) An international team of researchers using the NASA/ESA Hubble Space Telescope and several other observatories have, for the first time, uncovered a galaxy that is missing most -- if not all -- of its dark matter. This discovery of the galaxy NGC 1052-DF2 challenges currently accepted theories of and galaxy formation and provides new insights into the nature of dark matter.
