> There are eight planets in the solar system: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. The four inner planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars) fall under the category of terrestrial planets, while the outer four (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune) are the gas and ice giants.<div
