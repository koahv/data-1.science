#### Extract
>(Cognitive Neuroscience Society) People are using brain-machine interfaces to restore motor function in ways never before possible - through limb prosthetics and exoskletons. Technologies to repair cognition have been more elusive. That's rapidly changing with tools -- from fully implantable brain devices to neuron-eavesdropping grids atop the brain -- to directly probe the mind. These technologies, presented at the CNS conference in New York, are mapping new understandings of cognition and advancing efforts to improve memory in patients with cognitive deficits....
