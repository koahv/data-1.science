#### Extract
>(NASA/Goddard Space Flight Center) Much of Earth's biodiversity is concentrated in areas where not enough is known about species habitats and their wider distributions, making management and conservation a challenge. To address the problem, scientists used NASA satellite data to study cloud cover, which they found can help identify the size and location of important animal and plant habitats....
