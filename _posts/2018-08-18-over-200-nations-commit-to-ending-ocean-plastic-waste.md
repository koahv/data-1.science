Over 200 countries just signed a United Nations resolution in Nairobi, Kenya to eliminate plastic waste in the world&#8217;s oceans. The resolution is an important step towards establishing a legally binding treaty that would deal with the plastic pollution problem afflicting the world&#8217;s oceans. According to the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP), there will be more plastic by weight in the world&#8217;s oceans than fish by 2050 if current trends continue.
