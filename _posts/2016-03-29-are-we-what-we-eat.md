#### Extract
>(Molecular Biology and Evolution (Oxford University Press)) In a new evolutionary proof of the old adage, 'we are what we eat,' Cornell University scientists have found tantalizing evidence that a vegetarian diet has led to a mutation that -- if they stray from a balanced omega-6 to omega-3 diet -- may make people more susceptible to inflammation, and by association, increased risk of heart disease and colon cancer.
