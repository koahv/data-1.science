#### Extract
>(University of Lincoln) Scientists are developing new fleets of autonomous forklift trucks which can operate in warehouses alongside human co-workers to support tasks such as packing, palletising and transporting goods. As part of the ILIAD project, each robot is being developed to be 'human aware' -- equipped with advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to detect, track and predict the behaviour of humans and to plan movements based on the machines' own observations of warehouse lay-outs and patterns of activity....
